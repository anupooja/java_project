Anna's Restaurant

Founded in 2005 in Mumbai  as the first and only purveyor of Healthy Indian Takeout, Anna is celebrating 15 years in business this year.  While 2020 has not been a great year for anyone, we're proud to have persevered, and owe it to you, our loyal customers!

Over a decade ago, we partnered with the University of mumbai to become one of the few official, on-campus food service providers and caterers. 

We have since opened multiple campus locations, becoming experts in managing and operating in Educational and Corporate settings.  We pride ourselves not just in our high quality food offerings, but in unparalleled customer service.

Today, we still proudly offer our healthy Indian menu, in addition to a full range of chef-inspired takeout and catering services across campus, and to companies and schools throughout Mumbai.
